Frequently Asked Questions

How many hours a week can I save?

You may save anywhere from 5 to 15 hours every week using Backteams. You'll save even more time than your assistant does because we have a lot of expertise with administrative jobs and can usually perform them faster.

What's the best thing to outsource first?

Calendar management is a time-consuming duty, our clients (small company owners and executives) typically outsource them first. When you unload, you instantly get time back in your day. However, you can begin with whatever activity or project is most essential. You can learn more about what we do and schedule a call to discuss your unique requirements.

What if I'm too busy to coach someone?

That occurs. Fortunately, we can match you with a virtual assistant who is already skilled in the areas you want. Of course, you'll have to learn them the ropes of your unique firm, but the good news is that they've previously done comparable work for other customers and have a lot of experience servicing clients remotely.

Why should I pick Backteams?

If you want to work with a clever, experienced freelance virtual assistant who is trained and ready to assist you, resides in multiple countries, and has a college degree, Backteamsis the way to go. Our account managers do a fantastic job matching customers with the appropriate virtual assistant. We also manage your virtual assistant's workload so that you have regular availability. Working with a BackteamsAssistant rather than a simple Virtual Assistant is a no-brainer with 2-hour response times.

Can I talk to someone about my unique needs?

Without a doubt. We'd be delighted to speak with you. Many of our clients would rather have a consultation before enrolling. Ask us any more questions you may have, and tell us about your outsourcing requirements so that we can ensure there will be a BackteamsAssistant who can skillfully assist you.

How are Assistants selected and vetted?

We accept applicants as well as referrals from current Assistants. We exclusively hire Assistants with a college education, and some have a university degree as well as relevant professional experience. All Assistants are also subjected to a thorough background check.

How are the BackTeams support team trained?

Our team trains the whole Backteamssupport staff on the main administrative chores that clients want assistance with. We also teach them in more specific duties based on their expertise, preferences, and interests.

Will several Assistants or simply one person assists me?

You'll be collaborating with a single virtual support team representative. That implies there will be just one point of contact and one connection. This person will complete 95% of the job you require. However, if there is anything unique in which they are not an expert, they will seek assistance from a BackteamsAssistant who specializes in that field.

What abilities do the Backteamssupport team already possess?

Our support personnel and Assistants excel in email inbox management, appointment scheduling, social media management, email marketing management, research, invoicing, expenditure tracking, data entry, and other tasks. Contact us to discuss your requirements.

How does the pricing work?

Choose a monthly plan depending on the number of hours needed. Your virtual support team and Virtual Assistant will proactively ensure that they are assisting in extra areas to ensure that they are optimizing those hours. Hours do not roll over, but you can change your plan at any time.

What kind of long-term support do BackTeams provide?

The BackteamsSupport staff and Assistants have direct access to a Backteamsaccount manager, who consults with them on how to best serve their clients and proposes new methods to help. On-demand, we also assist in filling extra demands. If you need a new or different virtual assistant at any time, we are only an email away and can satisfy your recruiting needs fast.
